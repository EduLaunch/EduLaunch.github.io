Vietnam PolyEd REU

The Vietnam-PolyEd-REU has the overarching goal of furnishing Vietnamese undergraduate students with the essential training, competencies, and insights necessary for advancing in their professional trajectories within the domain of Education Science.

Comprising three key components, namely Mini-courses, Research, and Mentoring, the program begins with a series of mini-courses designed to enhance students' understanding of various research and education science topics. Subsequently, students will engage in the research phase, tackling accessible research problems under the guidance of experienced mentors, either local or international. These mentors will provide continuous support and direction throughout the project duration. Furthermore, participants will receive insights into graduate schools, academic life, and valuable advice on navigating the Ph.D./Master application process. Additionally, monthly meetings will be held to allow students to showcase their progress to the entire team. The mentorship aspect is intended to be enduring, with students encouraged to seek recommendation letters from their mentors as needed.

List of topics for REU 2024

Topic 1: Teacher education: Social Emotional Learning and Culturally Responsive Teaching (Mentor: Prof. Ximena Uribe-Zarain)

Topic 2: Teacher 4.0 from students' perspective: Who are they? (Mentor: Luong Dinh Hai)

Topic 3: How do students learn on social media? (Mentor: Luong Dinh Hai)

Topic 4: Curriculum development (Mentor: Nguyen Hong Lien)

Topic 5: Student's competency (critical thinking; historical thinking, problem solving; ...) (Mentor: Nguyen Hong Lien)

Topic 6: Classroom assessment, curriculum development, student competencies. (Mentor: Bui Thi Dien)

Topic 7: Education for sustainable development; General education program, textbook (Mentor: Nguyen Thu Ha)

Topic 8: Climate change, environmental education (Mentor: Nguyen Thu Ha)

Topic 9: Educational technology, technology learning design, emerging technologies (VR, AI,...) (Mentor: Tran My Ngoc)

Topic 10: Language education, applied linguistics, second language acquisition (Mentor: Tran My Ngoc)
